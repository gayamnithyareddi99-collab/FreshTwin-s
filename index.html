Core Intelligence Modules

Six Modules. One Complete PerishOps Intelligence Platform.

FreshTwin's combines Inbound Receiving Diagnostics, Real-Time Cabinet Intelligence, Shelf-Life DNA Fingerprinting, Spoilage Risk Scoring, Outcome-Learning Memory, and Federated Quality Benchmarking into one unified SaaS platform purpose-built for UK mid-market grocers and hospitality operators.

Module 01

Inbound-to-Inventory Intelligence

A first-of-its-kind framework bridging the gap between receiving-point environmental signals and real-time shelf-life outcomes, creating a continuous feedback loop.

The Inbound-to-Inventory engine ingests receiving diagnostics and real-time cabinet-level behavioural signals, parsing thermal nuances and mapping them into the proprietary Perishable Quality Ontology (PQO). This allows organisations to move beyond static temperature logs and capture "Shelf-Life DNA" that is often lost to staff turnover ensuring every delivery is fingerprinted for biological quality from the moment it arrives.
Module 02

Shelf-Life DNA Fingerprinting

Machine learning models identify and "fingerprint" thermal patterns of successful cold-chain journeys, benchmarking and cloning optimal storage logic across diverse sites.

The Quality DNA Fingerprinting engine uses deep-learning thermal modeling and microbial decay analysis to detect cumulative thermal exposure, humidity sensitivity, and recovery lag after door-open events. These risk profiles are mapped to personalised inventory pathways instantly allowing the business to implement targeted, automated FEFO (First-Expired, First-Out) rotations that satisfy both food safety rigour and commercial profit simultaneously.
Module 03

Shelf-Life Delta Diagnostics (SDD)

Automatically diagnoses root causes of premature spoilage by analysing the delta between expected shelf-life and actual quality-loss velocity.

The SDD engine identifies compressor lag, high-frequency door friction, delivery dwell-time delays, and micro-climate hotspots before they cause spoilage events. Validated to detect pre-spoilage signals with 94% accuracy, it delivers a 65% reduction in manual quality-checking time by automatically identifying batches requiring immediate FEFO rotation. Real-time Spoilage Risk Reports are generated without any manual staff review.
Module 04

Outcome-Learning Perishable Memory

Waste events are transformed into "Quality Intelligence" using proprietary algorithms that map unsuccessful outcomes back to their original thermal profile.

Every spoilage event whether a batch of dairy reaching early expiry or a meat display exposed to compressor fatigue is mapped back to its receiving-point thermal fingerprint and stored as institutional "Perishable Equity." This ensures that expert quality-check logic is never lost when senior store managers or chefs depart, allowing new floor staff to reach specialist-level proficiency within 14 days of activation.
Module 05

Signal-to-Action Auto-Synthesis

Automatically deconstructs a single environmental signal into a complex, multi-step operational workflow removing the need for manual staff-to-data translation.

When a humidity spike, recovery lag, or compressor stress point is detected, the platform automatically synthesises a structured multi-step action plan for floor teams including immediate FEFO rotation priorities, markdown recommendations, and equipment escalation alerts. Coupled with Risk-Weighted Inventory Evolution, higher-risk product groups are automatically prioritised and refined by the "FEFO Pipeline" in real time.
Module 06

Federated Quality Logic Network (FQLN)

An architectural approach to federated learning enabling anonymous "Quality DNA" benchmarks across UK retailers collectively improving predictive models without sharing private data.

The FQLN enables anonymised sector-wide cold-chain efficiency insights that allow mid-market grocers to benefit from the intelligence typically reserved for "Big Six" supermarket chains. Trends learned from high-efficiency grocers in London can optimise "Receiving-to-Rotation" strategies for a mid-market retailer in the Midlands, creating a collective intelligence ecosystem that new players would have to start from scratch to replicate.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know
About FreshTwin's

What exactly is the "Shelf-Life Gap" that FreshTwin's solves?

+
The Shelf-Life Gap is the critical disconnect between what temperature monitoring systems record (threshold alarms) and what actually determines a product's remaining sellable life (cumulative thermal exposure, micro-climate hotspots, door-open frequency, and biological decay velocity). Most UK food businesses rely on manual logging or basic sensors that only report if a fridge is above 8°C missing the 72% of quality loss caused by cumulative factors that occur within that threshold. FreshTwin's closes this gap by converting raw environmental signals into biological shelf-life intelligence.

How does FreshTwin's integrate with our existing refrigeration and EPOS systems?

+
FreshTwin's is built on a "Retrofit-Agnostic" architecture designed to sit above any underlying hardware system fridges, display units, prep stations, or EPOS/ERP retail stacks. Secure "Quality Logic Nodes" are deployed as retrofit IoT sensors that connect to your existing refrigeration assets without requiring replacement. A plug-and-play API platform built on dominant UK retail systems reduces onboarding timelines from weeks to hours. No equipment lock-in, no rip-and-replace.

What is "Interpretation Loss" and how does FreshTwin's prevent it?

+
Interpretation Loss is the institutional knowledge that disappears when an experienced store manager or head chef departs taking with them the specific "smell-test" logic they've built around which display units cause problems, which SKU categories are most sensitive, and which interventions actually protect quality. FreshTwin's captures this expert knowledge into structured Shelf-Life DNA a proprietary institutional asset the business owns permanently. New part-time floor staff can reach specialist-level proficiency within 14 days of system activation, compared to a historical average of 45 days.

Is FreshTwin's compliant with UK Food Standards Agency (FSA) and UK GDPR requirements?

+
Yes. FreshTwin's is strategically designed to align with the UK's evolving AI regulatory landscape, specifically the "Pro-innovation approach to AI regulation" set out by DSIT. The platform enables grocers to track, measure, and audit every automated quality interaction with high-fidelity logs and verifiable Quality Logic chains providing a robust, audit-ready trail for FSA regulatory inquiries and local Environmental Health Officers. All operational data handling adheres to strict ICO guidance and UK GDPR principles, with data captured locally via secure Quality Logic Nodes.

What results have pilot deployments achieved?

What is the Federated Quality Logic Network (FQLN) and how does it benefit my store?

+
The FQLN is a federated learning architecture that enables anonymised "Quality DNA" benchmarks from dozens of UK retail sites to constantly improve predictive models without sharing sensitive commercial data. Trends learned from high-efficiency grocers in London can optimise "Receiving-to-Rotation" strategy for a mid-market retailer in the Midlands creating a collective intelligence ecosystem.
